SASE Got Talent

WHAT: Social event showcasing the various talents of SASE students and professionals.
WHEN: March 21, 6:00pm-8:00pm
WHERE: Brazos Park
WHO CAN JOIN?: Professionals and students from all chapters accepted.
REGISTRATION: Opens January 27, 2020. Entries shall be on a first-come, first-serve basis.
DEADLINE: Registration - March 4, 2020 | Music Submissions - March 7, 2020

Judging Criteria

Acts shall be no longer than 5 minutes, not including transition time.

Performances shall be divided between Individual and Group categories, and will be evaluated based on cleanliness, technique, performance value, and originality/creativity.

Awards shall be given based on the criteria above.

Dress Rehearsal

No formal dress rehearsal scheduled, but the stage in Brazos Park shall be open from 5pm-6pm. Participants are welcome to use the stage and surrounding area as they wish. Please be courteous to other participants who would like to use the stage.

Cancellation Policy

In the event that participants would like to withdraw from the showcase after confirming their registration of interest, it must be no later than March 7, 2020.

A cancellation fee of $10 for individuals and $15 for groups, shall be required. These fees shall become donations towards a charity. [charity organization TBA]

Dressware

Attendees are encouraged to dress comfortably as we will be outside.
