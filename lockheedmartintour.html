SCRC Lockheed Martin Tour:

WHAT: SCRC Lockheed Martin and PAAN Company Tour
WHEN:Evening of March 20th
WHERE:Lockheed Aeronautics Base; 1 Lockheed Blvd, Fort Worth, TX 76108
WHO CAN JOIN?: Professionals and students from all chapters accepted.
REGISTRATION: Opens January 27, 2020. Entries shall be on a first-come, first-serve basis.
DEADLINE: Registration - Feb. 17th, 2020

Learn more about Lockheed Martin Aeronautics Base from Lockheed Martin and PAAN representatives

Registration

To confirm that you are going, payment will be collected to reserve a spot will be $20. After the tour concludes, a full refund will occur. This is to ensure limited spots for the tour are effectively utilized. If you cannot make it, you will not receive a refund. The tour spot is transferable to another individual (i.e. a substitution).

Substitutions may be made at no cost by providing the full name of original registered person, the full name of person replacing the original registrant, having the new person fill out the Google form. Email is acceptable; please email XXX with a subject line of: "ATTN: SCRC Tour Substitution" This information MUST be received by Monday, February 17, 2020 at 8:00 AM CST
